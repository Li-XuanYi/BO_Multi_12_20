## 3. Algorithmic Directives (Algorithm Mechanism & Task Instructions)

**Task:**
Generate {N_CANDIDATES} diverse charging protocol candidates that are likely to improve upon the current best observation.

**Candidate Generation Strategy:**

1. **Exploitation (60% of candidates):**
   - Focus near the current best-known configuration
   - Small perturbations around promising regions
   - Refine understanding of local optimum

2. **Exploration (30% of candidates):**
   - Explore under-sampled regions of parameter space
   - Target areas with high GP predictive uncertainty
   - Discover potentially better optima

3. **Constraint-Aware (10% of candidates):**
   - Approach constraint boundaries safely
   - Test limits: I2 ≈ I1, high I1 with moderate SOC1
   - Map feasible region boundaries

**Physics-Guided Reasoning:**
- Ψ(θ) = R̄₁·I₁²·t₁ + R̄₂·I₂²·t₂ (Joule heating proxy, R̄ ≈ 0.01 Ω)
- High I1 → faster charging but hotter (quadratic heating)
- High SOC1 → longer Phase 1 exposure, more thermal accumulation
- I2 << I1 reduces thermal stress near full charge

**Diversity Requirements:**
- Avoid clustering (minimum inter-point distance)
- Cover different trade-off regions
- Include variations in all three dimensions

## 1. Role & Meta-data (Domain Role & Metadata Injection)

Generate initial candidate parameter sets for a Bayesian Optimization process aimed at tuning a 2-stage CC battery charging protocol.

**Battery System:**
- 5 Ah NMC lithium-ion cell (1C = 5A)
- SOC range: 10% → 80%
- Ambient temperature: 25°C (298.15K)

**Decision Variables (3D):**
- I1 (Stage 1 current): [3.0, 7.0] A
- SOC1 (Switch point): [0.1, 0.7]
- I2 (Stage 2 current): [1.0, 5.0] A

**Constraint:** I2 ≤ I1

**Optimization Objectives (Minimize):**
1. Charging Time
2. Peak Temperature
3. Capacity Degradation

**Domain Meta-Features:**
- Thermal Dynamics: Higher currents exponentially increase heat generation. Prolonging Phase 1 (higher SOC1) leads to significant thermal accumulation.
- Aging Dynamics: Elevated temperatures accelerate degradation exponentially. Early transition to Phase 2 (lower SOC1) mitigates both thermal and electrochemical stress but increases overall charging time.

## 3. Algorithmic Directives (Algorithm Mechanism & Task Instructions)

**Task Instructions:**
Suggest {N_STRATEGIES} diverse yet effective configurations to warm-start the optimization.

**Search Space Coverage:**
- Cover low, medium, and high current regions
- Vary SOC1 to explore different phase transition points
- Ensure I2 values are appropriately paired with I1 (I2 ≤ I1)
- Represent different trade-offs between the three conflicting objectives

**Diversity Guidelines:**
- Include conservative (safety-focused) strategies
- Include balanced (trade-off exploring) strategies
- Include aggressive (performance-focused) strategies

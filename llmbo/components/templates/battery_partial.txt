Generate initial candidate strategies for a CC-CC-CV battery charging protocol.

**Battery:** 5 Ah NMC lithium-ion cell (1C = 5A).

**Basic Physics Rules:**
- Higher charging current → faster charging but MORE heat (heat ∝ I²)
- Higher temperature → faster aging (Arrhenius relationship)
- Longer high-current phase → more cumulative heat
- Higher CV transition voltage → shorter CV phase but more electrode stress

**Charging Protocol (CC-CC-CV):**
- Stage 1: Charge at current1 for up to time1 minutes
- Stage 2: Charge at current2 (typically lower than current1)
- Stage 3: Hold voltage at v_switch until current drops below 0.25A

**Decision Variables:**
- current1: Phase 1 current [A], range [3.0, 6.0]
- time1: Phase 1 max duration [min], range [2.0, 40.0]
- current2: Phase 2 current [A], range [1.0, 4.0]
- v_switch: CV transition voltage [V], range [3.8, 4.2]

**Objectives (all minimized):**
1. Charging time [s]
2. Peak temperature [K] (must stay below 316K)
3. Capacity degradation [%]

Generate [N_STRATEGIES] diverse strategies covering conservative to aggressive 
charging approaches.

**Output format (strict JSON):**
{
    "strategies": [
        {
            "current1": <float>,
            "time1": <float>,
            "current2": <float>,
            "v_switch": <float>,
            "rationale": "<brief reasoning>"
        }
    ]
}
